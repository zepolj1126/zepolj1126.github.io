UX writing

Remember when software used to come with a manual? Now, that manual is written right into the application itself.

I use language that guides users through the application in an intuitive and consistent way. Language is one of the most important design tool we have.

Work sample 1

Before

Error - Network Issue

We see you are using browser extensions (or other security-related tools) that will interfere with the completion of this form. Please disable them, reach out to your IT admin, or call Billing directly at 888-377-7707 ext. 3.

After

Let's try this again

It looks like your browser has extensions or other security-related tools that are blocking this form.

Please turn them off, or call Evercast support at +1-888-377-7707, Ext. 1.
