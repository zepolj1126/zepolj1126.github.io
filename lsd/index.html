About Us

A multidisciplinary architecture studio dedicated to collaborative craft and timeless design.


Lee Simon Design is an award-winning architecture practice recognized for their thoughtful designs across multiple sectors. With projects ranging from private estates to prominent masterplans, Laney LA seeks to create work that speaks powerfully - sparking a dialogue between enduring architecture and its evolving environment.

Based in the Hermosa Beach Arts District, Lee Simon Design is a place where diverse minds gather, committed to fostering both a critical discourse and a vibrant culture. With an exceptional roster recruited from around the world, Lee Simon Design has built a team united by a shared conviction - to make an impact through the transformative power of the creative process. From Mexico to New Zealand with specialties in landscape, product design, interiors, and architecture, Laney LA’s team leverages generous collaboration to craft meaningful spatial experiences. Ultimately, the studio has created a unique architectural process - one rooted in both conceptual rigor and an embrace of the individual dreams of every client.
